Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)


Q: Can I use my personal or work email?
A: Generally a work email is preferred so as to ensure proper tracking and security of the ticket.


Q: What is the purpose of this app?
A: We help with any technical related issues with your IBM Laptop and wifi connection in your local CIC.


Q: Who reviews my tickets?
A: We have a dedicated team of IT professionals that will track, review, and resolve the root cause of your issues in a timely manner.


Q: When can I expect to have my ticket resolved?
A: Tickets are usually resolved within 12-48 hours given the severity of the ticket.


Q: What happens if it turns out my ticket is not resolved?
A: The ticket will be escalated immediately if it turns out it does not meet your expectations, just submit another ticket, our goal is to have every employee satisfied!


Q: How do I navigate and use the app?
A: First you register your account with your local CIC location and other information, then you will login using your email, password, and username. Next you will visit the ticketing dashboard which allows you to choose and specify the technical issue that you are having from a dropdown menu. Should you choose you can also submit a custom ticket if your problem is not shown in the dropdown menu. Then you click submit and your ticket is immediately forwarded to the IT team at the CIC, which then you will be emailed regarding every status of your ticket.
